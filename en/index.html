Tentang Kami

PT. Faqib Surya Perkasa was established in 2018 with the aim of contributing to improving the quality of human resources, particularly in the oil and gas sector. and other training programs, enabling them to bring about significant change toward progress by developing a competitive advantage to achieve superior and optimal work performance.

By establishing harmonious communication and relationships, we will be the right partner in providing the best service and will be able to make a positive contribution to the company. We will also be able to accommodate the company's human resources in meeting career development needs, thereby increasing company benefits and customer value.

Visi

To become a training and certification provider that can provide professional services and is committed to developing human resources in accordance with their competencies and providing customer satisfaction.

Mission

  • Building a strong commitment by providing the best training and certification services.
  • To be a trusted partner for companies and organizations in developing human resources.
  • Make participants competent in their field of expertise.
